Machine Learning Research Engineer

View Publication (Soon)

The Challenge

Traditional clay extraction in mining is inefficient. This research explored using AI to predict clay mineral content from Mid-Infrared (MIR) spectral data to improve accuracy and efficiency.

My Contribution

  • Built and optimized a suite of production-ready models (Random Forest, SVM, CNN) on a dataset of over 55,000 spectral samples.
  • Engineered the complete end-to-end AI solution, from data preprocessing and augmentation to PyTorch CNN development and SHAP-based interpretability.
  • Deployed the final model in an interactive Streamlit application for real-time use by domain experts.

Results & Impact

  • The final model achieved a 92% R² score in predicting clay content.
  • Awarded the Gold Medal (1st Place) at the Pan-African Robotics Competition for contributions to ML and software engineering.
